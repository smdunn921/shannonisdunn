how do we keep going in the face of heartache and crisis?

they attack you. they come
for your families and homes.

my phone tells me i’ve spent
too much time on twitter.

you are told and shown that you
do not matter to any of them.

people are joking about which arm
to cut off so they don’t get drafted.

they spit on you as they take away
the rights you already don’t have.

people are celebrating the
mask mandate being lifted.

don’t dare take a step forward
or they will break your neck.

the military is underreporting how
many civilians they’ve murdered.

but you are fleeing your countries
and hoping you’ll stay alive.

and I am in the library, stressing
over essays of men who are long dead.
