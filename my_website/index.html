Shannon Dunn is an undergraduate at the University of Pittsburgh at Greensburg, majoring in English Literature, minoring in Creative and Professional Writing, and pursuing the Digital Studies Certificate. As of Spring 2022, they are the President of university's Gender and Sexuality Alliance, the Vice-President of the Alpha Alpha Delta Chapter of Sigma Tau Delta (the International English Honor Society) and a member of the university's President's Committee for Diversity and Inclusion, Nerdfighters, and Writing Club. Shannon has had work published in the University's Literary Magazine, Pendulum (Spring/Fall 2020 issue) and Phoenix of Alexandria's first issue of Rivets.
