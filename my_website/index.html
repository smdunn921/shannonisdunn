About Shannon

Shannon Dunn is an undergraduate English Literature major at the University of Pittsburgh at Greensburg, and is also pursuing the Digital Studies Certificate. As of Fall 2021, they are the Vice-President of the Alpha Alpha Delta Chapter of Sigma Tau Delta, the International English Honor Society, the President of university's Gender and Sexuality Alliance and a member of the university's Nerdfighters and Creative Writing Clubs. They is their second time being a teaching assistant in Coding and Digital Archives, the first time being Fall of 2020. Shannon has had work published in the University's Literary Magazine, Pendulum (Spring/Fall 2020) and Phoenix of Alexandria's first issue of Rivets.
