Shannon Dunn

Shannon Dunn is an undergraduate English Literature major at the University of Pittsburgh at Greensburg, and is also pursuing the Digital Studies Certificate. She is a member of the Alpha Alpha Delta Chapter of Sigma Tau Delta, the International English Honor Society, with whom she will be attending the International Convention in Las Vegas in March 2020 as a Digital Humanities roundtable participant. She also does the social media for the university's Gender and Sexuality Alliance and is a member of the university's Nerdfighters.
